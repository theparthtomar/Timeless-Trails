Understanding Indian States

India consists of 28 states, each with its own government and unique cultural identity. States play a major role in India's governance, with powers allocated to them under the Constitution of India. Learn about the history, geography, and significance of each state.

Understanding Union Territories

India's union territories are administrative regions governed directly by the central government. These territories play a strategic role in the country's administrative framework. Learn about each union territory, its governance, and its cultural importance.

The Indian Constitution

The Constitution of India defines the structure of governance and delineates the powers of the central and state governments. It is the supreme law of the country and plays a crucial role in maintaining India's federal structure.
